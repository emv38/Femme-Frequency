About

Our mission is to create a space where everyone can discover, connect, and be inspired by the incredible albums crafted by women artists and those from underrepresented backgrounds.

Tired of the same old playlists? Are Spotify's curated playlists becoming a bit too repetitive? Femme Frequency introduces a unique approach to music discovery. Simply tell us your mood, and we'll curate a personalized selection of albums that not only match your feelings but also showcase the rich and diverse sounds from underrepresented, lesser-known artists.

Impact

Why do we need Femme Frequency? "ArtRevi's 2018 Power 100 list of the “most influential people in the contemporary art world” included 40% women—a slight improvement from 2017 (38%) and 2016 (32%)". (Review)

"The NEA found that as women artists age, they earn progressively less than their male artist counterparts. Women artists aged 55-64 earn only 66¢ for each $1 earned by men." (National Endowment for the Arts)

Women and feminine bodies have been creating and producing art since the beginning of time. It's time we start paying attention.
